Tired of syncing and adding your files to Google Drive manually?

This application syncs your content with Google Cloud when your device is connected to internet. You will be able to choose what to sync and what not to sync. Never loose your irreplaceable data again!

Will be soon available for Windows and Linux PCs and for your Android. Keep Waiting!
