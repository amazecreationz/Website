Description
Tired of syncing Music, Videos and other Files between devices manually?

This application syncs your content when the devices paired are connected in the same network. There will also be an option to sync over internet if your devices are at two places and connected to internet (needs GDrive Sync).

Will be soon available for Windows and Linux PCs and for your Android. Keep Waiting!
