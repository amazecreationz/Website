Description
Useful for: National Institute of Technology Calicut students.
It calculates the Grade Point Averages of students from the Gradecard PDF obtained from Institute's website (DSS). The application has the capability to give semester wise as well as overall results. Credits accumulated in each semeter and total credits are calculated too.
Browse to your gradecard and hit calculate! More features coming soon.
